Privacy Policy

App: Estakada 99 TV
Last updated: March 6, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the Estakada 99 TV application ("the App") handles information when you use it on your Android TV device.

Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information. The App does not require you to create an account, log in, or provide any personal details.

What the App Does

Estakada 99 TV is a simple streaming application that plays a live audio/video stream from an internet radio station. When you open the App, it connects to the stream server to deliver content to your device. This connection is a standard network request and does not involve the collection of any user data beyond what is inherent in any internet connection (such as your device's IP address as seen by the stream server, which we do not control or log).

Third-Party Services

The App connects to the Estakada 99 streaming server to deliver live content. We do not use any third-party analytics, advertising, crash reporting, or tracking SDKs. No data is sent to any third party by this application.

Device Permissions

The App requests only the INTERNET permission, which is required solely to load the live stream. No other device permissions are requested or used.

Children's Privacy

The App does not collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever update this Privacy Policy, we will update the "Last updated" date above. Any changes will be posted at this same URL.
